Welcome to my little corner of the Interne

I’m Ashwin Patil. I am a sophomore year Computer Science undergrad from Pune, India. I love to read, code and sometimes write not necessarily in the same order.

I am passionately curious about everything in the universe, after all — The future belongs to the curious. The ones who are not afraid to try it, explore it, poke at it, question it and turn it inside out. It’s my curiosity that makes me an avid reader, amateur writer, and a better programmer.

I have a great passion for learning, of late it’s mostly concentrated around the world of computer sciences —including but not limited to Artificial Intelligence, Deep Learning, Machine Learning, and Competitive Programming. Being naturally curious I find great comfort in reading physics, fantasy and science fiction books.

“If I have seen further, it is by standing on the shoulders of giants.”— I firmly believe that all the advances in ideas, technology and knowledge are incremental and building on the work of genius thinkers is the best way to learn.
